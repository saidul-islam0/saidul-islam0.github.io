Content Team Head - Brainlytic: An Interactive Problem Solving App

2020 - 2024

Supervised by Dr. Anindya Iqbal, I was the volunteer Head of the Content Team of our interactive problem-solving website, Brainlytic. I was in charge of curating the problems and assigning responsibilities to the other Content Creators. Thus our site amassed over 700 problems in Math, Geometry and Logical Reasoning. The website is currently going through a massive overhaul.
