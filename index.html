About Me

Welcome! I am Saidul Islam, a passionate researcher with strong interests in Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Clinical Machine Learning, and Ethical AI. I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering from North South University , where I graduated with a score of 88% (3.44 / 4.00).

Currently, I am a Junior ML Developer at NIPRO JMI LTD, where I contribute to building applied machine learning solutions for medical diagnostics, with hands-on experience developing predictive models for diabetes risk assessment and tumor classification.

My core research focuses on advancing adversarial-resilient robust computer vision models, with a particular emphasis on fairness-aware, high-performance object detection for autonomous and security-critical systems. I am deeply interested in developing energy-efficient learning, focusing on pruning, compression, and edge-deployment strategies that push the boundaries of real-time intelligence.

Beyond academia, I am an avid traveler and passionate photographer. I have worked as a freelance photojournalist and I also enjoy horse riding. I genuinely enjoy insightful discussions on history, geography, theology and brainstorming business and entrepreneurship.

I am always open to collaborative in research opportunities and actively exploring research-based MS or PhD programs to further pursue my research ambitions and contribute to advancing the field.
